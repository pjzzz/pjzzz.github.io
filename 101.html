'You can pick up a copy at the spaceport,' said the old man. 'They've got racks of

'Oh,' said Arthur, disappointed. 'Well, isn't there anything that's perhaps a bit
more specific to me?'
'Everything you see or hear or experience in any way at all is specific to you. You
create a universe by perceiving it, so everything in the universe you perceive is
specific to you.'
