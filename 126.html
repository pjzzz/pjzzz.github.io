Ford looked around again. Then his attention was gradually drawn to Colin, the
absurdly over-happy security robot, who was cowering in a corner of the room gibbering
with what seemed strangely like fear.
Odd, thought Ford. He looked around to see what it was that Colin might have been
reacting to. Then he saw something that he hadn't noticed before, Iying quietly on top
of a work bench.
It was circular and black and about the size of a small side plate. Its top and its
bottom were smoothly convex so that it resembled a small lightweight throwing discus.
Its surfaces seemed to be completely smooth, unbroken and featureless.
It was doing nothing.
Then Ford noticed that there was something written on it.
Strange. There hadn't been anything written on it a moment ago and now suddenly there
