Preliumtarn
in

the
land

According to Prak, if Arthur's memory saved him right, the

guarded

Lajestic Vantrashell of Lob, and so, after a manner, it proved to be. He was a little man

"Keep to the left, please," he said,

left,"

little scooter.
They realized they were not the first to pass that way,
for
the path

led

His

At

they

as
not
to
spoil the Big Surprise!" it said on the reverse.
"Do you know what the message is?" they asked the wizened
little lady in the booth.
"Oh yes," she piped cheerily, "oh yes!"
She waved them on.
Every twenty miles or so
there

a
little
stone
hut
with showers and sanitary
facilities, but the going was tough, and the high sun baked down on the Great Red Plain,
and
the
Great
Red Plain rippled in the heat.
