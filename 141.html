'I see.'
Trillian gave Arthur a long look, and then, in a new tone
of voice, said, 'It's time for you to take responsibility, Arthur.'
Arthur tried to understand this remark. He found it often took a moment or so before
he saw exactly what it was that people were driving at, so he let a moment or two pass
at a leisurely rate. Life was so pleasant and relaxed these days, there was time to let
things sink in. He let it sink in.
He still didn't quite understand what she meant, though, so in the end he had to say
so.
Trillian gave him a cool smile and then turned back to the door of the hut.
'Random?' she called. 'Come in. Come and meet your father.'
