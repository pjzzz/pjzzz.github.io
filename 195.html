'Like the pikka birds.'
Ford had no idea what he was talking about and couldn't be bothered to ask. Instead he
said, 'So how the hell do we get out of this place?'
'Well I think the simplest way from here is just to follow the way down the valley to
the plains, probably take an hour, and then walk round from there. I don't think I could
face going back up and over the way I came.'
'Walk round where from there?'
'Well, back to the village. I suppose.' Arthur sighed a little
forlornly.
'I don't want to go to any blasted village!' snapped Ford.
'We've got to get out of here!'
