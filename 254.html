'Come on,' said Ford. He hurried on downstairs, with Arthur following nervously in
his wake. The encounter had shaken him badly and he didn't know why.
He didn't like places like this. For all of the dreams of Earth and home he had had
for years, he now badly missed his hut on Lamuella with his knives and his sandwiches.
He even missed Old Thrashbarg.
