helping ourselves to someone's scuppernongs was part of our ethical culture, but
money was different.

from Meridian to Maycomb Junction (a courtesy title- Maycomb Junction was in
Abbott County) where he had been met by Miss Rachel in Maycomb's one taxi; he
had eaten dinner in the diner, he had seen two twins hitched together get off the
train in Bay St. Louis and stuck to his story regardless of threats. He had
discarded the abominable blue shorts that were buttoned to his shirts and wore
