The aliens were looking at the shelves that held her CDs and her old records. One of
them nudged one of the others very slightly.
'Look,' he said. 'Elvis.'
Tricia stopped, and stared at them all over again.
'You like Elvis?' she said.
'Yes,' they said.
'Elvis Presley?'
'Yes.'
She shook her head in bewilderment as she tried to stuff
a new tape into her video camera.
'Some of your people,' said one of her visitors, hesitantly, 'think that Elvis has
been kidnapped by space aliens.'
'What?' said Tricia. 'Has he?'
'It is possible.'
'Are you telling me that you have kidnapped Elvis?' gasped
Tricia. She was trying to keep cool enough not to foul up her equipment, but this was
all almost too much for her.
'No. Not us,' said her guests. 'Aliens. It is a very interesting possibility. We talk
of it often.'
