Ford Prefect hit the ground running. The ground was about three inches further from
the ventilation shaft than he remembered it so he misjudged the point at which he would
hit the ground, started running too soon, stumbled awkwardly and twisted his ankle.
Damn! He ran off down the corridor anyway, hobbling slightly.
All over the building, alarms were erupting into their usual frenzy of excitement. He
dived for cover behind the usual storage cabinets, glanced around to check that he was
unseen, and started rapidly to fish around inside his satchel for the usual things he
needed.
His ankle, unusually, was hurting like hell.
