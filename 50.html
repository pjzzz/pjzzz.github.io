expense accounts. Ford Prefect's expense accounts were notoriously complex and
difficult
affairs and he had found, on the whole, that the lobby robots were ill-equipped to
understand the arguments he wished to put forward in relation to them. He preferred,
therefore, to make his entrance by another route.
