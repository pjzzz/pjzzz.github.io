The truth of the matter was that all sorts of highly dodgy stuff went on behind the
cheery, happy-go-lucky front that the Guide liked to put up - or used to like to put up
before this new InfiniDim Enterprises bunch marched in and started to make the whole
thing highly dodgy. There were all kinds of tax scams and rackets and graft and shady
deals supporting the shining edifice, and down in the secure research and data-
processing levels of the building was where it all went on.
Every few years the Guide would set up its business, and indeed its building on a new
world, and all would be sunshine and laughter for a while as the Guide would put down
its roots in the local culture and economy, provide employment, a sense of glamour and
adventure and, in the end, not quite as much actual revenue as the locals had expected.
