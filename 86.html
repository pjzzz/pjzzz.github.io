Arthur held up the unnailed end of the plank and the old
prophet scuttled into the recesses of his hovel, returning a moment or two later with a
small Sub-Etha radio. He turned it on, fiddled with the dial for a moment and put the
thing on the small wooden bench that he usually sat and prophesied on. He then took
hold
of the plank again and resumed hammering.
