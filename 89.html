Aunty Em ate nothing. She hadn't taken off her headdress, even to cook, and now she sat

sleepy, and I figured the least I could do was try to make small talk with our hostess.
"So, you sell gnomes," I said, trying to sound interested.

orders. Statuary is very popular, you know."
